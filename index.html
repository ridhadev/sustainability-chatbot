About This App

Our platform serves as a cutting-edge demonstration of the application of GPT (Generative Pretrained Transformer) technology to sustainability topic, specifically applied to ADNOC and UAE data.

It consists of the construction of a searchable knowledge base about sustainability vision and strategy adopted by the UAE, and ADNOC in particular.
